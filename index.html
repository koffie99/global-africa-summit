ABOUT US

The Global Africa Summit is a vehicle that will drive investment and collaboration between people and businesses of African origin and affiliation globally. The event will celebrate the successes of African undertakings in business and investment. It will spotlight personalities whose work brings growth to the African Agenda.

The Global Africa Summit will focus on business opportunities, business growth and trade expansion amongst entities in all 55 member states of the AU and Black Businesses in the United States. This new brand will continue the agenda of “for us, by us”
